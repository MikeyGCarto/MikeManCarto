About

About Me Hello! I’m Mikey, Mike, or Michael Grosspietsch, a recent graduate from the University of Wisconsin–Madison with degrees in both Human Geography and Cartography and Geographic Information Systems, now setting my sights on the professional world of GIS and geospatial technologies. While I may be new to the industry, my knowledge of geospatial collection and digital web mapping enables me to make complex GIS topics accessible to any audience. Before diving into this field, I spent several years working in the restaurant industry and home remodeling, where I honed my communication and problem-solving skills. Whether it's collaborating with a team or finding quick solutions in high-pressure situations, my experience has taught me how to connect with people from all walks of life and effectively solve workplace related problems. When I’m looking at the globe digitally, I’m often out exploring it in real life. From backpacking in the Grand Canyon to bouldering, camping, and hiking, I love spending time outdoors. I also have a bit of a nerdy side, and you might catch me playing Dungeons & Dragons, racing in Mario Kart, or attempting another diplomatic victory in Civilization 6. I’m excited to start my journey in the GIS world and look forward to where this path will lead. Feel free to reach out — I’m always happy to connect and chat about anything.
