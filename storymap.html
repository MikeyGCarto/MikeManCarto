Mad Max Meets Marvel

Michael Grosspietsch

For the client contract, I decided to create a map that combines the drab landscape of dry Australian Outback, unmaintained industry, and cartoons. This concept came from Junkertown, a cartoonish wasteland in post apocalyptic Australia. Overwatch 2 had recently come out, where Junkertown is from, and I was trying to rekindle my 14 year old excitement.

For this map, the rusted orange represents industrial zones, and the dried grass represents parks. I strove to mimic the grimey apocalyptic nature of Junker Town with the texture and color of parks and Industry. For the base color, I chose to create something on par with dry tall grass.

Since Junkertown is cartoonish in nature, I also added a dropshadow to the map, and made the water similarly cartoonish. These are seen when zoomed out, which helped juxtapose the concepts of cartoons and apocalypse in the theme.

Lastly, to end on a positive note, the font choice is key in showing the cartoonish nature of the maps theme. Purposefuly, it mimics the "Junkertown" written on the door of the reference pictures.
